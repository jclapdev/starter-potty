# Attachments

_Storage for images and files embedded in your notes._

## What this folder is for

When you paste or drag an image into a note, Obsidian saves the file here automatically and links it from your note. Keeping attachments in one folder stops them from cluttering the rest of the vault.

## What lives here

Image and media files referenced by your notes. You normally won't open this folder directly; it's managed for you.
