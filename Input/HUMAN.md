# Input

_A drop box for files you want the AI to read or process._

## What this folder is for

When you have something the AI should work from, a PDF, a spreadsheet, an export, a transcript, put it here and point the AI at it. Keeping raw inputs in one place keeps the rest of the vault tidy.

## What lives here

Empty by default. Add files when you have them; the AI reads from here and writes results into the right project note.
