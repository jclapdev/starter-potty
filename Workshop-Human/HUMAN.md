# Workshop-Human

_Your inbox. This is where you jot ideas for the AI to process: the human side of the workshop._

## What this folder is for

A friction-free place to dump ideas the moment you have them. You write messy; the AI turns the mess into organized, researched, actionable work. Point it here anytime by saying something like *"process my latest notes"* or *"look at Workshop-Human."*

## How to use it

1. Make a new note and dump ideas under `##` headings. Don't self-edit; that's the AI's job.
2. Tell the AI to process it.
3. Ideas become real work in the right place: a note in `Projects/`, research written into the relevant note, or a summary of what the AI did.

Nothing here is read by the AI unless you ask it to look. New to all this? The full walkthrough is in the `Start_Here/` folder.
