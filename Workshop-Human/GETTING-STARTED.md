## About this folder (`Workshop-Human/`)

This is your inbox. Jot ideas here the moment you have them, then point your AI at the folder and it turns them into real work. Use it however you like:

- Quick notes and half-formed ideas
- Links or reference material to process later
- Drafts you want to review before handing off to the AI
- Project seeds: one paragraph describing something you want to build

Nothing here is read by the AI unless you ask it to look at a specific file.
