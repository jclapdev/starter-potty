# AI-Workshop

_The AI's own working area._

## What this folder is for

Where the AI keeps its outputs, scratch space, and scheduled jobs, kept separate from your `Projects/` so its work-in-progress doesn't clutter yours.

## What lives here

- `Artifacts/`: finished outputs the AI produces
- `Projects/`: the AI's working notes for larger builds
- `Scheduled/`: definitions for tasks that run on a schedule
- `mcp-servers/vault/`: the navigation engine that keeps the system fast (infrastructure; you don't manage it)

You can look in here anytime to see what the AI is building, but you don't have to manage it.
