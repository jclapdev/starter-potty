# Systems

_How the vault itself works._

## What this folder is for

Reference notes on the rules and conventions that keep everything consistent: how a session runs start to finish, language and formatting rules, and naming conventions. These are the "operating manual" for the system.

## What lives here

Background documents the AI consults when it needs to follow a convention. You don't need to read them to use the vault, but they're here when you want to understand or change how something works.
