# Context

_The AI's brain. Everything it uses to stay consistent across sessions lives here._

## What this folder is for

This is where the AI keeps its skills, memory, history, and the maps that tie them together. It's what lets the AI pick up where it left off instead of starting cold every time.

## What lives here

- `Maps/`: index files for where things are and what the AI can do
- `Memory/`: durable preferences about how you like to work
- `Agents/`: background helpers for focused jobs
- `History/`: dated session notes and the open-work list
- `Skills/`: reusable, step-by-step processes
- `Systems/`: how the vault itself works

You rarely edit these by hand. The AI maintains them as you work.
