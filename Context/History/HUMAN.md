# History

_The running record of past sessions._

## What this folder is for

So the AI never starts cold. At the beginning of a session it reads the most recent note here and tells you what was left open, so you carry on instead of re-explaining.

## What lives here

- Dated handoff notes, one per session
- `open-work.md`: everything still in progress, in one list

The AI writes these when you say "wrap up." You can read them anytime to see what happened when.
