# Skills

_The AI's library of reusable, step-by-step processes._

## What this folder is for

When you do the same multi-step task often, it becomes a "skill": a written process the AI follows the same way every time. Skills are what make the AI get more reliable at your specific work over time.

## What lives here

One folder per skill, each with a `SKILL.md` the AI reads when that task comes up. To add one, say *"I want to turn this into a skill,"* and the AI will build and save it.
